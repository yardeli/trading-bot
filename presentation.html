Risk Management

VaR

Value at Risk

99% confidence, 3.5% daily limit. Historical simulation (non-parametric). Positions scaled down if breached.

Vol

Volatility Targeting

15% annualized vol target. Positions scaled by target/realized ratio. Keeps risk constant across regimes.

DD

Drawdown Protection

Progressive de-risking from 12.5%. Hard stop at 25%. All positions zeroed on breach.

Pos

Position Limits

20% max single name. 150% max gross leverage. 60% max net exposure. Herfindahl concentration check.

Corr

Correlation Crash Filter

Monitors cross-asset correlation. Reduces momentum exposure when correlations spike (crisis indicator).

Cost

Transaction Cost Modeling

Commission (1bp) + spread (2bp) + square-root market impact (Almgren-Chriss). Turnover penalty in optimizer.

Unified Dashboard

Backtest Mode

  • Press START to run 5-year walk-forward backtest
  • Live-streaming equity curve (Chart.js)
  • Real-time drawdown visualization
  • Performance metrics update as it runs
  • Model weight evolution
  • 113 rebalances with full trade log
  • Completes in ~2-3 minutes

Live Trading Mode

  • Connects to Alpaca paper trading ($100K)
  • Auto-rebalances (2 min to 1 hour interval)
  • Real orders: BUY/SELL with $amount and status
  • Live positions with P&L per position
  • Account equity, cash, buying power
  • Market open/closed status
  • Force Rebalance and Stop buttons
One interface, two modes. Same quant engine powers both. Toggle between Backtest and Live in the header.
